About LiteETA

What is LiteETA?

LiteETA is a simple, lightweight, and fast real-time bus tracker for the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority's (WMATA) Metrobus service. It displays information about Washington, DC Metrobus routes and schedules in a clean, accessible format.

Why did you make this?

The official WMATA BusETA site exists here. I made this site to improve on some of the shortcomings my friends and I experience on the official product. This includes:

  • Ability to add and remove favorites
  • Simple and easy to use interface
  • Responsive design for mobile

I sought to fix these problems by creating my own version of the web application. My goal was to demonstrate my programming skills and create something useful to me and my family.
